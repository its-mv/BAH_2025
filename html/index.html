About Us

Welcome to The MOONITORS — your gateway to the mysteries of the Moon. We're a team of space enthusiasts, developers, and researchers driven by a single vision: making lunar science accessible, interactive, and inspiring for everyone.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between space data and public understanding. With cutting-edge technology like 3D lunar modeling and image-based landslide detection, we bring the Moon closer to you — not just as a distant rock, but as a world full of wonder.

🔭 What We Do

  • ✨ Create immersive 3D Moon explorations using Spline-based visuals
  • 🧠 Detect and analyze lunar blunders & landslides using image AI
  • 📚 Educate through fascinating moon facts and visual insights
  • 🌍 Build awareness about past missions and future lunar explorations

We're not just a website — we’re a growing community of moon lovers, learners, and explorers. Whether you’re a student, a space nerd, or a curious dreamer, there's something here for you.
